Schema First (SDL)

Build your GraphQL API with ease by defining your Schema separately from the implementation, by using the SDL (Schema Definition Language).

Built on top of asyncio

We decided to build our engine on top of asyncio and enjoy all the benefits of it.

Tartiflette aiohttp

In addition to the Tartiflette engine, we provide a tartiflette-aiohttp distribution, which allowS you to expose your GraphQL API through HTTP.

Dev experience matters

We focused our energy on providing a simple and powerful developer experience, giving you the ability to extend your engine and develop quickly your GraphQL API. Try it !
